% Description: Table with Landscape Orientation

% Note
% 1. If there are too many columns in a table, it might be too wide for the page.
% 2. Display a table in landscape mode in such situations using rotating package.

\documentclass{article}

\usepackage{siunitx}
\usepackage{booktabs}
\usepackage{rotating}                                   % To display tables in landscape.

\sisetup{
  round-mode          = places, % Rounds numbers
  round-precision     = 2, % to 2 places
}

\begin{document}

\section{Tables in \LaTeX}

\subsection{Landscape tables}

Sometimes when displaying huge tables with many columns, it's better to display them in landscape mode.

\begin{sidewaystable}[h!]                                 % Just replace the table with the sidewaystable environment.
    \begin{center}
    \caption{Landscape table.}
    \label{tab:table1}
    \begin{tabular}{l|S|r}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Value 1} & \textbf{Value 2} & \textbf{Value 3}\\
        $\alpha$ & $\beta$ & $\gamma$ \\
        \midrule
        1 & 1110.1 & a\\
        2 & 10.1 & b\\
        3 & 23.113231 & c\\
        \bottomrule
  \end{tabular}
  \end{center}
\end{sidewaystable}

\end{document}
